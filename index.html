About me

I am a lecturer at the Faculty of Pharmacy, Lac Hong University. My research applies computer-aided drug design (CADD) and cheminformatics to accelerate the discovery of new therapeutic candidates. I focus on 3D pharmacophore modeling, machine learning, molecular docking, ADMET prediction, molecular dynamics simulations, and binding free energy calculations (MM/GBSA and MM/PBSA). I am also interested in integrating machine learning to improve predictive performance and automate compound screening pipelines. This page highlights my publications, projects, and computational tools developed for both research and teaching.
